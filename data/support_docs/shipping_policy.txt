# Shipping & Delivery Policy

## Shipping Options

### Standard Shipping
- Delivery Time: 5-7 business days
- Cost: $5.99 for orders under $50, FREE for orders $50+
- Available for all 50 US states

### Express Shipping
- Delivery Time: 2-3 business days
- Cost: $12.99
- Available for all 50 US states

### Next-Day Shipping
- Delivery Time: 1 business day
- Cost: $24.99
- Orders must be placed before 2 PM EST
- Not available for PO boxes
- Available only in contiguous US states

### International Shipping
- Delivery Time: 10-21 business days
- Cost: Calculated at checkout based on destination
- Available to most countries
- Customer responsible for import duties and taxes

## Order Processing

### Processing Time
All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the next business day.

### Order Confirmation
You will receive an email confirmation within 1 hour of placing your order. If you don't receive this email, please check your spam folder or contact customer support.

### Tracking Information
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. Tracking updates may take 24-48 hours to appear in the carrier's system.

## Delivery Issues

### Missing Package
If your package shows as delivered but you haven't received it:
1. Check with neighbors and building management
2. Look for a delivery notice
3. Wait 24 hours (packages sometimes show delivered early)
4. Contact our support team with your order number

### Damaged Package
If you receive a damaged package:
1. Take photos of the damage (box and contents)
2. Contact our support team within 48 hours
3. We'll arrange a replacement or refund

### Wrong Item Received
If you receive the wrong item:
1. Do not use or discard the item
2. Contact support with photos of what you received
3. We'll send the correct item and arrange return pickup

## Shipping Restrictions

### Items That Cannot Be Shipped
- Hazardous materials
- Perishable goods
- Items requiring special handling

### Address Requirements
- Complete street address required
- APO/FPO addresses supported
- PO boxes supported for standard shipping only

## Holiday Shipping

During peak holiday seasons (November-December), please allow additional 2-3 days for delivery. Order early to ensure timely delivery for holidays.
